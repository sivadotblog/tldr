The first public beta of iOS 27 is now available. It works on every iPhone that runs iOS 26, but Apple Intelligence features will require an iPhone 15 Pro or newer. The update is focused on performance, stability, and making the iPhone feel even better to use. The biggest addition is Siri AI, which can now hold an ongoing conversation, understand onscreen context, take actions in apps, and answer broad questions.

### Science & Futuristic Technology

[### SpaceX is gearing up for Starship's 13th test flight later this week (9 minute read)](https://arstechnica.com/space/2026/07/spacex-is-gearing-up-for-starships-13th-test-flight-later-this-week/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

The next Starship test flight could take off as soon as Thursday. The mission will be similar to the last Starship flight in May, but there will be real Starlink satellites inside the vehicle's cargo bay. The Starlink V3 satellites will not be a part of SpaceX's operational network. SpaceX engineers will attempt to briefly establish laser communication links between the Starlink V3s and other spacecraft flying in low-Earth orbit, after which they will fall back into the atmosphere and burn up during reentry.

[### Meet the newly public, Bezos-backed company that's recreating the sun and stars (9 minute read)](https://sherwood.news/markets/general-fusion-spac-debut-nuclear-fusion-greg-twinney/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

The firm uses a hybrid approach called Magnetized Target Fusion, which combines magnetism and shockwaves to generate intense heat and compress atoms. Its goal is to have a fusion power plant running by 2035. This article features an interview with its CEO, Greg Twinney, where he discusses the company's roadmap.

[### Control the ideas, not the code (7 minute read)](https://antirez.com/news/169?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

Many programmers are not as impactful as they could be because they are looking at the code. Looking at the code is suboptimal and often pointless. The amount of code that LLMs generate makes it impractical to read, understand, and edit every line of code. It's more efficient to just control the ideas - how things work, what is the best design, and how to reach a certain level of performance - and then check the implementation for correctness.

[### Apple's New Speech API vs Whisper: The First Real Benchmark (9 minute read)](https://get-inscribe.com/blog/apple-speech-api-benchmark.html?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

Apple's SpeechAnalyzer cuts word error rate compared with SFSpeechRecognizer by 3.5x to 4x on the same audio. It beats Whisper Small by a comfortable margin with roughly a third of the compute time per second of audio. SpeechAnalyzer is now the strongest on-device option for English on Apple hardware. Whisper covers far more languages and runs anywhere, but the days of it being the automatic accuracy pick are over.
