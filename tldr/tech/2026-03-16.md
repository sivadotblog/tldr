### Programming, Design & Data Science

[### The "Night Shift" Agentic Workflow (10 minute read)](https://jamon.dev/night-shift?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

This post outlines an agentic workflow that allows developers and agents to work together around the clock. The developer takes the 'day shift', while AI agents work overnight. The developer prepares as much as they can during the day shift for the agents to work autonomously as they rest, finishing tasks by the next morning. The workflow has helped the author spend less time babysitting and much more time thinking about the problems they need to solve.

[### Comprehension Debt — the hidden cost of AI generated code (8 minute read)](https://addyosmani.com/blog/comprehension-debt/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

Comprehension debt is a hidden cost to human intelligence and memory that results from excessive reliance on AI and automation. It is the growing gap between how much code exists in a system and how much of it any human being genuinely understands. It can lead to teams not being able to explain why design decisions had been made or how different parts of the system are supposed to work together. The debt has to be paid sooner or later, and interest accrues rapidly.
