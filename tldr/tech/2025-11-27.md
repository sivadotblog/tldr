On November 4, astronauts on the Tiangong space station discovered that one of the two docked ferry ships had a damaged window. The astronauts will return next year, soon after the arrival of their replacements.

[### Why (Senior) Engineers Struggle to Build AI Agents (6 minute read)](https://www.philschmid.de/why-engineers-struggle-building-agents?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

Transitioning from deterministic systems to probabilistic agents requires trading certainty for semantic flexibility. People trained on strict interfaces will find this uncomfortable. However, forcing an agent into a deterministic box defeats the purpose of using one. Instead of coding away ambiguity, start engineering systems that are resilient enough to handle it.

[### MIT study finds AI can already replace 11.7% of US workforce (6 minute read)](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/26/mit-study-finds-ai-can-already-replace-11point7percent-of-us-workforce.html?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

MIT has released a study that shows that AI can already replace 11.7% of the US labor market across finance, health care, and professional services. The study used a labor simulation tool called the Iceberg Index to simulate how workers interact across the country and how they are affected by AI and corresponding policy. The index offers a detailed map of where disruption is forming, down to the zip code. It is designed to provide a skills-centered snapshot of what today's AI systems can do to give policymakers a structured way to explore scenarios before they commit real money and legislation.
