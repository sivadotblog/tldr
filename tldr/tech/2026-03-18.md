[### Amazon rolls out 1-hour, 3-hour delivery as ultrafast shipping trend grows in the US (3 minute read)](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/17/amazon-rolls-out-1-hour-3-hour-delivery-in-latest-fast-shipping-test.html?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

Amazon is rolling out one-hour and three-hour deliveries in parts of the US. Three-hour delivery will be available in about 2,000 cities and towns, while one-hour delivery will be available in hundreds of areas. More than 90,000 products are eligible for the service. Shoppers will be able to filter search for products that can be delivered in three hours or less.

[### OpenAI launches GPT-5.4 mini and GPT-5.4 nano on APIs (2 minute read)](https://www.testingcatalog.com/openai-launches-gpt-5-4-mini-and-gpt-5-4-nano-on-apis/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

OpenAI has released GPT-5.4 mini and GPT-5.4 nano. GPT-5.4 mini is live on the API, Codex, and ChatGPT, while the nano model is reserved for API users only. Both models are designed for workflows where response time shapes the product. GPT-5.4 mini is priced at $0.75 per million input tokens and $4.50 per million output tokens, while GPT-5.4 nano lands at $0.20 and $1.25.
