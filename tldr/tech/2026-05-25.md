### Science & Futuristic Technology

[### Inside the World's Biggest Bet on Fusion Energy (3 minute read)](https://www.cnet.com/science/climate/inside-the-worlds-biggest-bet-on-fusion-energy/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

The International Thermonuclear Experimental Reactor (ITER) is the world's biggest bet on fusion energy. At an estimated cost of $22 billion, ITER is a donut-shaped vacuum chamber designed to contain temperatures 10 times hotter than the core of the Sun. The project is so daunting in scale that longtime geopolitical rivals are collaborating on the project, pooling their resources to share in its potential risks and rewards. This article provides an overview of the project and contains a video showing what the heart of the experiment looks like.

[### China launches Shenzhou-23 mission with potential record one-year stay in orbit (2 minute read)](https://au.finance.yahoo.com/news/china-launches-shenzhou-23-mission-022212095.html?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

China's Shenzhou-23 mission sent three astronauts to the Tiangong space station on Sunday. One crew member may remain in orbit for a full year, the longest human space mission in China's history. The astronaut selected for the extended stay will be determined later during the mission. The mission will also conduct the first autonomous rapid rendezvous and docking procedure with Tiangong's core module, a capability expected to support future lunar operations.
