### Science & Futuristic Technology

[### FAA proposal: Supersonic airliners can fly over US cities if they're quiet (3 minute read)](https://arstechnica.com/gadgets/2026/07/faa-proposal-supersonic-airliners-can-fly-over-us-cities-if-theyre-quiet/?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

The US Federal Aviation Administration has proposed a new rule that would allow commercial supersonic airliners as long as they can reduce the ground-level impacts of their sonic booms. The new rule would require any sonic boom overpressure at the surface to be kept below 0.11 pounds per square foot. Critics of the new rule say it doesn't actually consider loudness or annoyance. Legalization of quieter supersonic flights doesn't guarantee a successful comeback for commercial supersonic airliners - Concorde's massive fuel consumption made it difficult to sustain profitable operations.

[### Career advice in the age of AI (11 minute read)](https://x.com/philhchen/status/2072793818945167475?utm_source=tldrnewsletter)

The valuable work of the next decade is everything that can't be graded within the span of model training. Focus on resources that are truly limited and learn to find problems in addition to solving them. Work on the most ambitious form of a problem and learn to execute well in the last mile. Put yourself in the right position to see opportunities. The key to unlocking opportunity is to focus on finding interesting problems and delivering extraordinary results.
