[### Apple's AI Game is Misunderstood (14 minute read)](https://davefriedman.substack.com/p/apples-ai-game-is-misunderstood?utm_source=tldrai)

Some say Apple is behind on AI, while others say Apple is playing a different game. The gap between these two realities is where investment risk lives. Apple is likely building a moat where inference is free, privacy is structural, and the capex already happened when users bought the hardware. The next three years will tell us whether that's wisdom or complacency.

[### RL Environments for Agentic AI: Who Will Win the Training & Verification Layer by 2030 (29 minute read)](https://www.datagravity.dev/p/rl-environments-for-agentic-ai-who?utm_source=tldrai)

[### Retiring GPT-4o and Older Models (5 minute read)](https://openai.com/index/retiring-gpt-4o-and-older-models/?utm_source=tldrai)

GPT-4o, GPT-4.1, GPT-4.1 mini, and certain other older models will be retired on February 13 to streamline capabilities and transition users toward newer generations like GPT-5 and beyond. GPT-4o in ChatGPT had been restored after earlier deprecation due to user feedback, but the final retirement reflects broader adoption of more capable models. While the change affects the consumer ChatGPT experience, there are currently no simultaneous API changes, and developers relying on older models will be guided to migrate to newer alternatives as part of normal deprecation practices.
