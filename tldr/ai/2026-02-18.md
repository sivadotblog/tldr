ERL trains policies with an explicit attempt ‚Üí feedback ‚Üí reflection ‚Üí revised attempt loop, then reinforced the successful revision back into the base model. The approach improves sparse-reward learning and tool-using reasoning performance while keeping deployment-time inference cost unchanged.

[### Open-Web Simulator for Agent Training (22 minute read)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.14721?utm_source=tldrai)

WebWorld uses a pipeline of 1M+ open-web interactions to simulate long-horizon (30+ step) browsing tasks, paired with a multi-metric WebWorld-Bench for intrinsic evaluation. Trajectories synthesized from the simulator boosted downstream web-agent performance and transferred to other domains like code, GUI, and games.
