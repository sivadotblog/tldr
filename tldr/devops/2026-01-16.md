[### What we know about Iran's Internet shutdown (5 minute read)](https://blog.cloudflare.com/iran-protests-internet-shutdown/?utm_source=tldrdevops)

Internet traffic from Iran dropped to effectively zero on January 8 amid widespread protests, following a 98.5% decline in IPv6 address space and a 90% fall in overall traffic from major providers. As of January 10, the country remained almost entirely cut off from the global internet, mirroring past government tactics during periods of unrest.
