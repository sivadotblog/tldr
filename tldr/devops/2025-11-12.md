[### Measuring characteristics of TCP connections at Internet scale (12 minute read)](https://blog.cloudflare.com/measuring-network-connections-at-scale/?utm_source=tldrdevops)

TCP connections on the Internet are highly diverse, with most carrying very few packets and bytes while a small fraction handle massive volumes, creating a heavy-tailed distribution across packet counts, bytes sent, connection duration, and request counts. Metrics like packet ratios, path MTU, and initial congestion window reveal asymmetries and performance characteristics that influence throughput, latency, and efficiency across global networks.
