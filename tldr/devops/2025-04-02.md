### News & Trends

[### Amazon EKS now enforces upgrade insights checks as part of cluster upgrades (1 minute read)](https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2025/03/amazon-eks-enforces-upgrade-insights-check-cluster-upgrades/?utm_source=tldrdevops)

Amazon EKS has introduced a new control to prevent accidental cluster upgrades when issues that could impact application compatibility are detected. This feature uses EKS upgrade insights to automatically check for potential upgrade problems. An override option is available for specific cases like development environments.

[### AWS Identity and Access Management now supports dual-stack (IPv4 and IPv6) environments (1 minute read)](https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/whats-new/2025/03/aws-identity-access-management-dual-stack-ipv4-ipv6-environments/?utm_source=tldrdevops)

AWS IAM now supports a dual-stack public endpoint, allowing connections over IPv6, IPv4, or both. Customers can also access it privately via AWS PrivateLink. The feature enables a smooth transition from IPv4 to IPv6.

[### Temporal Raises $146M Series C to Power the Future of Durable Applications (2 minute read)](https://temporal.io/blog/temporal-series-c-announcement?utm_source=tldrdevops)

Temporal has secured $146M in Series C funding led by Tiger Global which values the company at $1.72B post-money. Over the past 18 months, Temporal's open-source adoption increased by 600% to 183,000 weekly active developers deploying the platform across 7 million clusters, while Temporal Cloud's revenue grew 4.4x with adoption by 2,500 customers.

### Opinions & Tutorials

[### Why we need short-lived credentials and how to adopt them (9 minute read)](https://www.hashicorp.com/en/blog/why-we-need-short-lived-credentials-and-how-to-adopt-them?utm_source=tldrdevops)

Short-lived "dynamic" credentials that automatically expire after minutes or hours are replacing static secrets to minimize credential theft. Canva adopted a centralized secrets manager after teams had to halt development priorities to rotate credentials. A roadmap for transitioning to dynamic secrets includes standardizing authentication methods, measuring and communicating the benefits, and monitoring Vault metrics.

[### SaaS Is Broken: Why Bring Your Own Cloud (BYOC) Is the Future (4 minute read)](https://thenewstack.io/saas-is-broken-why-bring-your-own-cloud-byoc-is-the-future/?utm_source=tldrdevops)

Traditional SaaS models are becoming unsustainable due to high, unpredictable costs and limited data control, especially in data-intensive areas like AI and observability. Bring Your Own Cloud (BYOC) offers a compelling alternative by allowing companies to run SaaS applications on their own infrastructure, combining the simplicity of SaaS with the control, cost-efficiency, and compliance benefits of self-hosted solutions.

[### How to create an effective paging strategy (6 minute read)](https://www.datadoghq.com/blog/on-call-paging/?utm_source=tldrdevops)

Datadog's paging strategy focuses on refining paging to highlight user-impacting issues and establishing clear on-call responsibilities to reduce burnout and enhance response times. It streamlines workflows by integrating incident tracking and communication tools across platforms, empowering engineers to efficiently resolve critical problems and maintain focus on high-priority tasks.

The solution will leverage Google Cloud's Asynchronous Replication for efficient data protection, minimal downtime, and seamless disaster recovery management.
