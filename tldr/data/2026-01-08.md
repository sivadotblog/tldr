GraphRAG enhances conventional RAG by building a hierarchical knowledge graph from raw documents, enabling both granular local retrieval and global corpus-level reasoning for LLMs. GraphRAG synthesizes high-level insights and reduces hallucinations by using token-overlapping chunking, entity resolution, weighted relationships, and recursive community detection. [### Context is the next data platform, and why context graphs are key to understanding processes (5 minute read)](https://www.glean.com/blog/context-data-platform?utm_source=tldrdata)

Context graphs help AI understand how work actually gets done by capturing real processes, relationships, and activity across enterprise tools, not just static data. By learning the repeated steps and patterns of work, AI agents can automate tasks more reliably and act in ways that match how an organization really operates. This makes context graphs a core part of a new data platform built specifically for agent-driven automation, not reporting.
