### Attacks & Vulnerabilities

[### Orange Belgium discloses data breach impacting 850,000 customers (2 minute read)](https://www.bleepingcomputer.com/news/security/orange-belgium-discloses-data-breach-impacting-850-000-customers/?utm_source=tldrinfosec)

Attackers breached Orange Belgium's IT systems in July, stealing personal data of 850,000 customers, including names, phone numbers, SIM card numbers, PUK codes, and tariff plans. The company detected the breach at the end of July and confirmed that passwords, emails, and financial information were not compromised. Orange Belgium identified the threat group responsible, but hasn't disclosed its identity. The company confirmed this was unrelated to China's Salt Typhoon attacks targeting telecoms worldwide.

[### Apple Patches CVE-2025-43300 Zero-Day in iOS, iPadOS, and macOS Exploited in Targeted Attacks (1 minute read)](https://thehackernews.com/2025/08/apple-patches-cve-2025-43300-zero-day.html?utm_source=tldrinfosec)

Apple has patched CVE-2025-43300, a zero-day exploit in the ImageIO framework that causes memory corruption via malicious images. It affected iOS 18.6.2, iPadOS 18.6.2/17.7.10, and macOS Ventura 13.7.8, Sonoma 14.7.8, and Sequoia 15.6.1. This is Apple's seventh zero-day patch in 2025.

[### TPG Telecom estimates 280K affected by subsidiary breach (2 minute read)](https://www.theregister.com/2025/08/20/tpg_telecom_iinet_breach/?utm_source=tldrinfosec)

TPG Telecom's subsidiary iiNet suffered a data breach due to a stolen employee login, exposing the data of around 280,000 customers. The exposed information includes emails, phone numbers, addresses, and some setup passwords. The breach was contained, and affected customers are being notified.

### Launches & Tools

[### RingReaper (GitHub Repo)](https://github.com/MatheuZSecurity/RingReaper?utm_source=tldrinfosec)

RingReaper is a post-exploitation agent for Linux that utilizes io\_uring to minimize chances of being detected by EDR solutions.

[### BamboozIEDR (GitHub Repo)](https://github.com/olafhartong/BamboozlEDr?utm_source=tldrinfosec)

BamboozIEDR is a comprehensive ETW (Event Tracing for Windows) event generation tool for testing and research purposes.

[### Chalk (GitHub Repo)](https://github.com/crashappsec/chalk?utm_source=tldrinfosec)

Chalk captures metadata at build time and can add a small 'chalk mark' (metadata) to any artifacts so that they can be identified in production. It can also extract chalk marks and collect additional metadata about the operating environment during this process.

Microsoft's Copilot AI allowed users to access files in M365 without leaving audit log records, creating potential security and compliance risks.
